Steve Jobs

1955-2011

"Design is not just what it looks and feels like. Design is how it works"

Biography

Steve Jobs was a visionary entrepreneur who co-founded Apple Inc. and transformed the tech industry. Born on February 24, 1955, in San Francisco, California, he was adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs. In 1976, he co-founded Apple with Steve Wozniak, introducing the Apple I computer. After a power struggle, Jobs left Apple in 1985, but returned in 1997 and led the company to become one of the most valuable in the world. Under his leadership, Apple introduced the iMac, iPod, iPhone, and iPad, revolutionizing technology and design. Jobs passed away on October 5, 2011, leaving behind a legacy of innovation.
